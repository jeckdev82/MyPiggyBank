# My Piggy Bank 🐷

**My Piggy Bank** is a simple and fun iOS app to manage your personal savings goals.
Create virtual piggy banks, set a target amount, and watch your savings grow — one coin at a time.

---

## Features

- Create multiple piggy banks with custom names, icons and colors
- Set a savings goal and track your progress
- Add deposits with coin and banknote denominations
- View your transaction history
- Animated savings containers (pig, jar, safe, chest and more)
- Unlock unlimited piggy banks with a one-time premium purchase

## Requirements

- iOS 26,2 or later
- iPhone 

## Privacy

My Piggy Bank does not collect any personal data. All your savings data is stored locally on your device.
