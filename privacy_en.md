# Privacy Policy — My Piggy Bank

*Last updated: March 29, 2026*

## 1. Overview

My Piggy Bank is a personal savings app. This Privacy Policy describes how user data is handled.

## 2. Data Collection and Storage

**My Piggy Bank does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data entered in the app (piggy bank names, amounts, goals, transactions) is stored **exclusively on the user's device** via SwiftData. Under no circumstances is this data sent to third-party servers.

## 3. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

In-app purchases are managed through **RevenueCat**. RevenueCat may collect and process certain technical data (e.g. anonymous device identifier, purchase history) required to verify and restore purchased licenses.

For more information, please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy:
[https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## 4. Apple Diagnostic Data

The app may benefit from anonymous crash reports provided by iOS through Apple's diagnostics program, if the user has consented in the device settings. This data is managed entirely by Apple and is not accessible to the developer in identifiable form.

## 5. Children

My Piggy Bank does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. Since no user account or personal data collection is required, the app is safe for all age groups.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be published on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend checking it periodically.
